About Us

GURUKUL EDUCATION was established in 2076. Gurukul is not just an institution but a big educational shelter for students, a helping hand, a provider of friendly advice blended with quality education and providing total solutions in the field of education.

Our experienced teachers have produced hundreds of successful students throughout their teaching careers. Weekly classes, intensive revision classes and private home tuition are available. The structured support that we provide our students will enable them to achieve great results in their exams and have the confidence to take on new challenges throughout their life.
